Our City

Davao is the largest city in the world (244,000 hectares), which is considered as the Orchid Capital of the Philippines.
It is also famous for fruits and as a take-off point for climbing Mt. Apo, the Philippines' highest mountain and habitat of the
monkey-eating Philippine eagle. It has a mild pleasant climate all year round and is shielded from typhoons. Some reasons why
people should visit Davao are out of this world escapades, has a rich culture, provides sumptous food and there are a lot of people.
said to be out of this world escapades because Davao offers different kinds of attractions that would cater to everyone's taste.
Davao city has a rich culture because there are also a number of museums and craft centers in the area where you could experience
and relive history first-hand. Furthermore, Davao is known for delicious food because it is mostly surrounded by waters makes fresh
and raw seafood available everywhere inexpensively. But if an authentic local dining experience is what you looking for, there is a
market at Davao called Roxas Night Market, vendors sell everything from finger food to complete meals freshly prepared before your eyes
at a very low cost. Additional to that, you can also visit the Philippines' first Chocolate museum in Malagos Garden Resort and try
Davao's award-winning bean-to-bar cacao treats. Lastly, Durian is one of the fruits that you can eat in Davao before leaving because
it has a truly unique taste that might or might not satisfy your taste buds.
