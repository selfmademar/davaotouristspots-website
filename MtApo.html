Mountain Apo

Mount Apo, also known locally as Apo Sandawa, is a large solfataric, dormant stratovolcano on the island of Mindanao, Philippines. With
an elevation of 2,954 meters (9,692 ft) above sea level, it is the highest mountain in the Philippine Archipelago and located between Davao City
and Davao del Sur province in Region XI and Cotabato in Region XII. The best time to visit this attracion during November to May to avoid crowds is
early in the morning before 10:00am. But this attraction is closed from June to August. Hiking and mountaineering are some of the activities tourists
can do. For more details, you can call +63 (082) 553 1441-Davao del Sur tourism, +63 998 950 2558, +63 917 556 0499-Mt. Apo Adventures (tour operator).
