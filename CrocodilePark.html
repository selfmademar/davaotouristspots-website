Crocodile Park

The Davao Crocodile Park is an establishment that showcases a state of the art crocodile farming system in the Philippines. It has
the most up-to-date crocodile farm design complete with modern facilities and equipment. The park educates people about the natural world of
crocodiles and their connections with nature. They also provide information on the other wild animals in Davao Crocodile Park. Apart from the
creative variety of crocodile-themed foods and products, the reptiles themselves are a grand sight to see. Accompanied by a local, English
speaking guide, you'll see a number of fine specimens, especially Pangil, who is known as the largest saltwater croc in captivity. Opening
hours of Crocodile park is from 8:00am-6:00pm. Entrance fee varies depending on the visitor. P250.00 for adult on Friday, Saturday and Sunday
and P300.00 at 3:00pm. Places they can visit are Butterfly House, Tribu K'Mindanawan and Crocodile Park. While for kids age 2-12 years old,
P150.00 on Friday, Saturday and Sunday and P175.00 at 3:00pm having on the mentioned places.
