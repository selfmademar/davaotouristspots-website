Samal Island

Samal or officially called "Island Garden City of Samal" is a 4th class component city of the province of Davao del Norte. It is made up of
Samal Island and the smaller Talikud Island in the Davao Gulf. It is part of the Metropolitan Davao area and two kilometers away from Davao City, the
largest city and the primary economic center of Mindanao. The best time to go in Samal Island is during sunny weather almost daily from December to May.
It does experience rain showers during the monsoon months of June to November so make sure to check weather updates first before planning your activities
and booking your flights to the Philippines or flight to Manila. Moreover, there are also some other spots that you can go and do different activities in
Samal Island such as taking a dip at Hagimit Falls, exploring the island while riding on a motorcycle, explore Monfort bat cave, spelunking, visiting Pearl
Farm beach resort, lounge at Kaputian Beach, go hiking at Mt. Puting Bato, experience thrills at Maxima Aquafun, scuba diving adventure, catch a sight of
the Vanishing Island, explore Talikud and cliff diving. For other inqueries, you can visit "https://guidetothephilippines.ph/trips-and-experiences/davao-tours"
