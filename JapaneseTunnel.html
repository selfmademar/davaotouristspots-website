JAPANESE TUNNEL

As you approach the tunnel, among the initial things that you can see are attractive statues, together with that of a
Planet War II Japanese soldier and a Japanese woman clad in a kimono. Throughout the tunnel you can expect to also locate quite
a lot of statues of Japanese soldiers too. D' Japanese Tunnel is located along the South Diversion Road, in Hillcrest Subdivision,
Matina Balusong, Davao City. It is about 30 minutes from downtown Davao by car. Since it is located just outside a subdivision,
it is not hard to find a taxi to take you back to the city or your hotel. For more updates, you can visit
"https://www.agoda.com/d-japanese-tunnel-family-resort/hotel/davao-city-ph.html?cid=1844104"
